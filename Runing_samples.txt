For data process of S3Rec/SASRec/CATSR:


For SASRec and S3Rec (Single market):
1. Adjust the model_name [option:SASRec,S3Rec,UniSRec] and the dataset[option:de,jp,...] in finetune.sh
2. Run:  bash finetune.sh

For SASRec-bert and UniSRec-bert:
Pretrain:
1. Adjust the model_name  and the dataset in pretrain_dist.sh for distrubuted training
2. Run: bash pretrain_dist.sh   (for distrubuted training)
Or 
1. Run python pretrain.py --model_name=UniSRec  (for single gpu training)

Finetune:
Adjust the model_name [option:SASRec,UniSRec] and the dataset[option:de,jp,...] in finetune.sh
bash finetune.sh
